## Identity, in one paragraph
Kaab Ashqeen is an applied data and ML builder in Austin, TX. The archive
moves between three voices: graduate-ML student (perceptrons, FCNs, RL,
optimization, SVD), product builder (YGOPLUS as the headline), and
recruiting candidate prepping for data-science / FDE / quant roles. The
through-line is "learn it, then ship something that uses it."

## Voice and rhythm

- **Prompt cadence:** median ~150 chars, mean ~1,300 chars. Short when
  stuck, long when planning.
- **Style:** lowercase, casual, direct ("how should i prep for ds
  interviews"). Polished only when drafting public material.
- **Tone:** earnest, curious, allergic to hand-wavy answers. Asks "what do
  I actually do next?" a lot.

## Site implications

1. The hero leads with **applied data + ML builder**, not anything
   clinical. The archive supports it.
2. About should hold both halves — graduate ML coursework *and* shipped
   product — without making either feel like a hobby.
3. Projects: YGOPLUS as the headline; ML notebooks and quant experiments
   as supporting evidence; ashqeen.com itself as a small information-design
   piece.
4. Voice: warm, plain, lowercase-friendly. Avoid recruiter clichés
   ("passionate," "results-driven"). Keep sentences short.
