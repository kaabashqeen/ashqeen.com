YGOPLUS

A Yu-Gi-Oh! deck testing and analysis tool built around competitive player workflows: card search, deck construction, probability tools, endboard helpers, pricing context, accounts, and subscriptions. Built end-to-end and iterated mostly through prompted code, with hand-tuned UX. The deepest case study on this site.
